Our Mission

To provide comprehensive education in Animal and Environmental Biology, fostering research excellence and practical skills for sustainable environmental management and conservation.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in biological sciences, producing graduates who are innovators and leaders in environmental conservation and animal welfare globally.
